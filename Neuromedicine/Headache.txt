Headache
What is the most likely diagnosis?
A) Migraine
B) Tension headache
C) Cluster headache
D) Temporal arteritis (Giant cell arteritis)
Correct Answer: D) Temporal arteritis (Giant cell arteritis)
###
What is the treatment for temporal arteritis?
A) NSAIDs
B) Oral corticosteroids (prednisolone)
C) Sumatriptan
D) Verapamil
Correct Answer: B) Oral corticosteroids (prednisolone)
###
Which of the following is the drug of choice for prophylaxis of episodic and chronic
cluster headache?
A) Amitriptyline
B) Greater occipital nerve block
C) Verapamil
D) Valproic acid
Correct Answer: C) Verapamil
